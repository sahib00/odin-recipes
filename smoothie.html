SUMMER IN A SMOOTHIE

DESCRIPTION

With Bananas,Raspberry,Pomegranate and Oranges,this smoothie captures the best flavors of summer in one delicious cup.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 large ripe Banana
  • 1.5 cups of unsweetened rasphberry thawed.
  • Half cup Pomegranate juice.
  • 3/4 cup Orange juice.
  • 3/4 cup Almond breeze unsweetened original Almond milk.

DIRECTIONS

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until creamy and smooth,scraping down sides as needed.
  2. Taste and adjust flavour as needed.Add more unsweetened original Almond Breeze or fruit juice if too thick.If not sweet enough, add more banana
